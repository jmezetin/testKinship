Getting Involved

Kinship is a completely volunteer-led organization. This community is successful because of the hard work of many volunteers who operate at different levels. Kinship welcomes its membership and supporters to get involved with Kinship wherever they may be.

Coordinator Responsibilities

  • Liaison between the chapters, groups and members within the region
  • Reports regularly to the Member Services Committee concerning the activities of the region
  • Planning, motivating and providing resources to the chapters
  • Revitalizes dormant chapters
  • Organizes new chapters in the region

The following positions are open and available.

Common Kinship Activities

  • Monthly Vespers A guest speaker brings a spiritual message, can be followed by fellowship & refreshments
  • Monthly Luncheon Lunch at a local restaurant on the weekend, may be after Church Service. All are encouraged to attend, whether they have attended church or not.
  • March in the Pride Parade - An Outreach Event Decorate vehicle with Kinship Banner. Vehicle has Kinship Website address local Kinship contact information Toss candy to the crowd during the parade Have flyers and cards about SDA Kinship
